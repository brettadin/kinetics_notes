Formula Sheet — Section 5 (IDs & quick use)

  • Eq.5.1.1 homogeneous catalytic saturation: \( r=\dfrac{k_2 K_1 C_T [A]}{1 + K_1[A]} \)
  • Eq.5.2.1 Michaelis–Menten: \( v=\dfrac{V_{\max}[S]}{K_m+[S]} \)
  • Eq.5.2.2 turnover & efficiency: \( k_{\text{cat}}=V_{\max}/E_T \), \(k_{\text{cat}}/K_m\)
  • Eq.5.2.3 Lineweaver–Burk: \( 1/v=\dfrac{K_m}{V_{\max}}\dfrac{1}{[S]}+\dfrac{1}{V_{\max}} \)
  • Eq.5.2.4 Eadie–Hofstee: \( v = -K_m\,(v/[S]) + V_{\max} \)
  • Eq.5.2.5 Hanes–Woolf: \( [S]/v = [S]/V_{\max} + K_m/V_{\max} \)
  • Competitive: \( v=\dfrac{V_{\max}[S]}{\alpha K_m+[S]} \)
  • Uncompetitive: \( v=\dfrac{V_{\max}[S]}{K_m+\alpha'[S]} \)
  • Pure noncompetitive: \( v=\dfrac{(V_{\max}/\alpha')[S]}{K_m+[S]} \)
  • Mixed: \( v=\dfrac{(V_{\max}/\alpha')[S]}{(K_m/\alpha)+[S]} \)
  • Eq.5.4.1 Langmuir isotherm: \( \theta_A=\dfrac{K_A P_A}{1+\sum K_i P_i} \)
  • Eq.5.4.2 LH rate: \( r=\dfrac{k K_A K_B P_A P_B}{(1+K_A P_A + K_B P_B)^2} \)
  • Eq.5.4.3 ER rate: \( r=\dfrac{k K_B P_B P_A}{1+K_B P_B} \)
